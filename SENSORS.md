# Sensor Explanations

## RFID Module (RC522)
The RFID reader uses radio frequency to detect tags or cards. When a card is presented, it reads the unique ID and compares it with authorized IDs to grant access or confirm presence.

## IR Sensor
Infrared sensors detect motion or presence by measuring changes in infrared radiation. Used near the bed to detect if the owner has entered the room.

## Gas Sensors
- MQ-2: Detects LPG, CNG, smoke, and other combustible gases. Ideal for kitchen leak detection.
- MQ-135: Sensitive to CO2, ammonia, and other gases. Used for air quality monitoring.

## Water Level Sensor
Measures the water level in the tank using conductivity or ultrasonic principles to control the motor.

## Motion/Presence Sensor (PIR)
Detects movement in the garden to prevent sprinkler operation when someone is present.

All sensors connect to the microcontroller's digital or analog pins for real-time monitoring.
